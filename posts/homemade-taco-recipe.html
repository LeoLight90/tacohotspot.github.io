Easy Homemade Taco Recipe

By Taco Hotspot

If you love tacos but don’t want to go out, here’s a simple recipe to make delicious tacos at home:

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or chicken
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the meat in a skillet until browned.
  2. Add taco seasoning and a little water, simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm the tortillas.
  4. Assemble with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and your favorite salsa.

Enjoy your homemade tacos!
