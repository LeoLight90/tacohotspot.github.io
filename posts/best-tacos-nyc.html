Top 5 Taco Spots in NYC

By Taco Hotspot

New York City is known for its diverse food culture, and tacos are no exception. Here are our top 5 must-try taco spots:

  1. Los Tacos No. 1 – Authentic Mexican street-style tacos.
  2. Taqueria Diana – Known for their Al Pastor and fish tacos.
  3. Empellón – A modern twist on traditional flavors.
  4. Casa Enrique – Michelin-starred Mexican cuisine.
  5. La Esquina – Hidden gem with a cool vibe.

Each of these spots brings something unique, from hand-made tortillas to bold, spicy salsas.
